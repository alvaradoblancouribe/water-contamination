FDA regulations

The FDA regulates water bottles like they are food. It does not have a mandatory testing program, therefore there is no garantee that the bottle of water is safe.

EPA

The EPA is in charge of regulating tap water and is funded by tax-payer money. However, some of the national testing of chemicals in public water systems is never made public. The strictness of their regulations on tap water differs greatly depending on who is holding the presidential office.
