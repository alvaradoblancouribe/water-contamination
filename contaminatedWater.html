Harm of Water Contamination

Many of the contaminants that are found in water can't be smelled, seen, or tasted. These contaminants can lead to acute and chronic health risks. If you live close to an agriclutural or industrial area, chemicals in the water can increase lead to cancer, reproductive problems, and impair eye, liver, kidney, and other body functions

These are some common waterborne contaminants:

Arsenic

Long term effects: Skin, bladder, lung, kidney, nasal passages, liver and prostate cancer. In addition to cardiovascular, pulmonary, immunological, neurological and endocrine disruption effects
