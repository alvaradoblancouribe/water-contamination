Given the vast amount of contaminants that can be found in tap water, there is a high demand for products that decrease the amount of contaminants in drinking water. Therefore, the water industry is always evolving to provide people with as much “clean” water as possible. Currently, two of the most popular solutions are water filters and water bottles.
